About ICTAZ

The Information technology association of Zambia is a professional regulatory body of the field of ICT in Zambia. It was approved by an act of parliament. Its responsibiliy is to maintain and regulate ICT proffession and to ensure they work up to standard and according to provided ethics.

The mukuba University chapter is a student chapter is an affileate member of the ICTAZ and its mission is to provide knowledge skills training and exposure of student members to the professional field of ICT as well as the vast opportunities that are available.
